## Propaganda Persuasion Technique Classification

This literature review surveys psychology, communication research, computational social science, political communication, propaganda studies, rhetoric, discourse analysis, media framing, moral psychology, sentiment analysis, emotion classification, stance detection, and natural language processing for media analysis. The goal is to define a non-redundant and usable taxonomy for classifying persuasion techniques, moral framing, and intended reader emotion in media texts, then use those categories to summarize an actor's agenda or stance toward an institution.
